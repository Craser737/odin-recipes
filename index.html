Odin Recipes

Ancient Norse names are all the rage, but getting your little Odin or Freya to eat shouldn't make you feel enraged! Here are 3 recipes guaranteed to get those little gods eating their vegetables and gaining more power!

Berserker's Butternut Squash and Coconut Curry Soup

Perfect for to hiding those veggies, this warm and comforting dish is blended for those littles who struggle with new textures. A soup so good Thor will wish his hammer was a spoon.

Bjorn's Bolognese

Bjorn loves his noodles, and we love getting protein into our little warriors. this recipe does just that with a delicious, hearty meat sauce that doesn't skimp on the Veggies.

Toril's tortellini Soup

This soup, is known in certain circles as simply "The Soup." It is packed with flavour and includes sausage, tomatoes, and your favorite tortellini.

Leek Bacon and Potato Soup

Another good soup for getting vegetables into your kids.

Auntie B's Curried Shrimp

This is the curried shrimp recipe that my family has had every Christmas eve since I can remember. Over 40 years! It isn't Christmas without this.
