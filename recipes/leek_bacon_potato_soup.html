Leek Bacon Potato Soup

  • 1 tbsp butter
    • 4 thick bacon strips
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 300g/small leak, sliced and well washed
  • 5-6 yellow potatoes, peeled and cubed.
  • 1L "Better than Bouillon" vegetable stock and .5L water
  • OR 1.5L vegetable stock
  • 250ml >18% cream
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in large pot on medium high, then add bacon and onion. Stir occasionally until bacon and onion are golden.
  2. Add leek and potatoes. Stir well, cover and reduce heat to medium. Cook for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in stock and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a low simmer. Simmer for 20-30 minutes. Ensure vegetables are soft.
  4. Fish out about two cups of potato cubes and set aside. Blend remainder of soup until well smooth.
  5. Return potatoes previously removed, add cream and season to taste will salt and pepper.
