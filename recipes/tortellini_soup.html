Toril's tortellini Soup

Description

This fast, creamy and hearty soup is sure to impress the adventurous among your Skalds and Shieldmaidens.

We use the Instant Pot pressure cooker in this recipe, but it can be easily adapted to use a conventional stove-top method. We advise using mild sausage for the littles, but for those more daring in palate, hot italian sausage is delightful.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 15 fl-oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 10 oz fresh tortellini
  • 1/2 cup whipping cream
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach

Spices

  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Select SAUTE and set to HIGH Instant Pot. Add olive oil.
  2. Once hot, add onions and carrot. Stirring occasionally, cook for 5-7 minute until onion is translucent. Add garlic and cook for 1 additional minute.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, vegetable stock, and spices.
  4. Close lid and set the vent to SEALING. Select the PRESSURE COOK/MANUAL setting and set cook time to 1 minute on high pressure.
  5. Once the program ends, allow a 5 minute natural release then rotate the vent to VENT and release the remaining pressure.
  6. Carefully remove the lid, select SAUTE, add the tortellini and stir occasionally for 2 minutes. Add cream and continue to simmer for an additional 3 minutes.
  7. Select KEEP WARM function, add baby spinach and cover with lid. Allow spinach to wilt for a couple of minutes. Serve warm and enjoy!
