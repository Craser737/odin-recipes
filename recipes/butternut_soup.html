Berserker's Butternut Squash and Coconut Curry Soup

Description

Hearty vegetables, warm spices, and coconut milk are blended to create a rich and creamy soup.Blended for those littles who struggle with certain textures.

This recipe can be made in an Instant Pot pressure cooker for those with little time, or on the stove for those old-school Norse-folks.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp live oil
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 4 large carrots, chopped
  • 1 butternut squash, cubed
  • 2 celery sticks, chopped
  • 5 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped (optional)

Spices

  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop onions, celery, and carrots. Peel and cube squash into 1" pieces. Mince garlic and ginger.
  2. In a large pot on medium high (Instant Pot saute high setting) add olive oil, onions, and salt. Saute onions until translucent 5-7 minutes.
  3. Once onions are translucent, add ginger and garlic and saute for 1 minute.
  4. Add carrot, squash, and celery. Continue to saute for an additional 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. tip in vegetable stock and add remainder of spices. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a low simmer (Instant Pot: Pressure cook, select high and set time to 5 minutes. Allow 10 minute natural release once program ends.) Simmer until vegetables are soft.
  6. Using an emersion blender (or conventional blender if unavailable) blend mixture until smooth.
  7. Stir in can of coconut milk and adjust salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with a dash of cilantro if your little table Loki likes it.
